Research Methods and Statistical Techniques

 

 

Course Number

Semester

Credits

MLIS-104

I

4

 

Faculty profile: Vinit Kumar (Ph.D)

Objectives of the Course:

o   To understand the research methods and process.

o   To understand qualitative and quantitative techniques for data analysis and consolidation.

o   To develop the skills for report writing.

Course content:

Unit-1

o   Research: Concept and process of research(PPT)

o   Types of research

o   Historical background of LIS research

o   Research design, Hypothesis

o   Ethical aspects of research

Unit-2

o   Research methods:

o   Scientific Method

o   Historical Method

o   Descriptive Method

o   Survey Method

o   Case study Method

o   Experimental Method

Unit-3

o   Research Techniques and Tools

o   Questionnaire and Schedule

o   Interview

o   Observation

o   Scales and Check lists

o   Sampling Techniques

Unit -4

o   Data Analysis and Interpretation

o   Descriptive statistics: Measures of central tendency; Mean, Median and Mode, etc.

o   Tabulation and generalization

o   Graphical presentation of data using statistical tools, Excel/SPSS

Unit-5

o   Research report writing: structure, and formats

o   Style manuals - Chicago, MLA, and APA

o   Bibliometrics: Concept and Definition and Use in LIS

o   Indian Copyright Act and IPR

o   Plagiarism and Fair Use.

Method of Teaching: Lectures, brainstorming Sessions, Seminars, etc.

Method of Assessment: Assignments, Presentations, written exams

Recommended Readings

1.       Kothari, C.R.,1985, Research Methodology- Methods and Techniques, New Delhi, Wiley Eastern Limited.

2.       Kumar, Ranjit, 2005, Research Methodology-A Step-by-Step Guide for Beginners, (2nd.ed.), Singapore, Pearson Education.
